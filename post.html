The Ministry of Basic and Secondary Education Inaugurates New Advisory Council on Education

The Honourable Minister for Basic and Secondary Education, Madam Claudiana A. Cole Presided over the inauguration of a New Advisory Council on Education.

The Advisory Council is composed of the (5) Regional Governors , Permanent Secretary, Ministry of Higher Education, Research, Science and Technology, Deputy Permanent Secretaries of the Ministry of Trade and the Ministry of Women, Children and Social Welfare, Veteran Educationists ( former School Principals and experienced civil servants ), representatives of the Kanifing Municipal Council and the Banjul City Council , President of the Gambia Teachers' Union, and other key partners of the Ministry of Education.

The Advisory Council is charged with the responsibility to advice the senior management on various issues within the Education Sector.
